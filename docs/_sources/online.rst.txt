===================
Office 365 - Online
===================

This guide explains how to install the **IBM Envizi Emissions API** add-in from an XML manifest file on
**Office 365 Online**.

.. note::
   Office 365 Online provides the most straightforward method for sideloading the add-in.

1. Go to `Excel <https://excel.cloud.microsoft/>`_
2. From the **Home** tab, click Add-ins
3. Click **More Add-ins**

.. image:: _images/add-in.png
   :alt: Add-ins menu in Excel
   :align: center

4. Click **Upload My Add-in**

.. image:: _images/my-add-in.png
   :alt: Upload My Add-in option
   :align: center

5. Click **Browse**
6. Find the **manifest.xml** you downloaded
7. Click **Upload**

.. image:: _images/upload.png
   :alt: Uploading the manifest file
   :align: center

8. Confirm the installation. A new **IBM Envizi Emissions API Add-in** button will appear in the Excel ribbon (typically under the **Home** tab).

.. image:: _images/installed.png
   :alt: Installed add-in in Excel ribbon
   :align: center
