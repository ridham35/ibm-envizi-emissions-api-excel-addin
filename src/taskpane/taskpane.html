Getting started

Get started with the Emissions API Excel add-in. You can use this add-in with our Excel template, or your own spreadsheet.


1. Choose a template (optional)

In the Excel template, select the appropriate tab for your emissions type.

2. Enter your activity data

In the input table, add data like fuel use and type, location and the date range.

3. Calculate emissions

Once you've inputted your data, you can use the add-in functions to calculate your emissions in the output table.
